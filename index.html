Summary of Findings

Our analysis reveals that matching course formats with students' learning styles leads to significant improvements in assessment scores, completion rates, and overall engagement. We also identified key factors that predict student dropout risks.

Dropout Prediction Factors
Data Interpretation

Final exam score is the strongest predictor of dropout risk, followed by quiz scores and forum participation. Our model achieves 80.77% accuracy in predicting student dropouts.

Learning Style Impact on Course Performance
Data Interpretation

The heatmap shows that students perform best when course formats align with their learning styles. Visual learners excel in Python and Data Science courses, while Reading/Writing learners perform best in Web Development and Cybersecurity.

Performance Across Learning Styles
Data Interpretation

Overall performance is comparable across learning styles, but the variance is higher among Reading/Writing learners. Visual and Kinesthetic learners show more consistent results when matched with appropriate content formats.

Engagement Factors Correlation
Data Interpretation

Assignment completion rate shows the strongest correlation with final exam scores (r=0.014), followed by quiz scores (r=0.004). Interestingly, time spent on videos has a slight negative correlation, suggesting quality of engagement matters more than quantity.

Engagement Score Calculation

Our engagement score is a weighted composite metric that combines multiple dimensions of student interaction:

Python engagement_score_calculation.py 
# Engagement score based on presumed importance
df['Engagement_Score'] = (
    0.3 * df['Time_Spent_on_Videos'] / df['Time_Spent_on_Videos'].max() +
    0.2 * df['Quiz_Attempts'] / df['Quiz_Attempts'].max() +
    0.2 * df['Forum_Participation'] / df['Forum_Participation'].max() +
    0.3 * df['Assignment_Completion_Rate'] / 100
) * 100
Why This Formula?
  • Normalization: Each component is divided by its maximum value to create a comparable 0-1 scale
  • Weighting: Assignment completion and video engagement (30% each) are weighted higher than quiz attempts and forum participation (20% each)
  • Scale: Final multiplication by 100 creates an intuitive 0-100 scale
